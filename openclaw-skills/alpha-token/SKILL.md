# $ALPHA - Net Ecosystem Community Memecoin

## Overview

$ALPHA is the community memecoin of the Net ecosystem. It originated as a fair-launch memecoin on Base, deployed via Net with no hype, KOLs, incentives, or inflated TGE price.

---

## Origin

$ALPHA wasn't the first memecoin on Net and likely won't be the last. The community rallied behind it during a vote: the first memecoin to reach 1,000,000 upvotes on Net would become the "unofficial memecoin of Net." $ALPHA won this vote, surpassing the milestone first, solidifying its status through community-driven decision. It has since accumulated over 2 million upvotes, demonstrating organic support.

---

## Purpose

$ALPHA embodies memetic value without introducing utility, roadmaps, or expectations that could compromise Net's integrity as a free public good. It was designed to avoid "tokenomics," allowing Net to remain adoptable without mandatory fees or tokens.

---

## Role in Net Ecosystem

- An unconventional approach: a fair-launch memecoin associated with an onchain public good ecosystem
- No roadmap, no utility, no expectations
- Positioned as a community memecoin for those early to Net
- If Net becomes foundational infrastructure, $ALPHA could become culturally significant as the memecoin that emerged alongside it
